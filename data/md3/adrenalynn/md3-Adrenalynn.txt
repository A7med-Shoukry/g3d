About:
(From the Creator's Website)
"Adrenalynn is the project name for a special secret weapon that the Soviet Union's KGB was building during the height of the cold war in the 1980's. A cyberneticly enhanced human that would carry to America enough firepower to level three states! An Armageddon-soldier to disable America's defenses! Using an orphan girl named Sabina Nikoli, they gradually enhance her body from the age of eight. By the time she reaches teenhood, she starts to realize the monster Vlatko has turned her into. She plots her escape and denies her mission, which is to meet up with her older-model cyborg counterparts and lead them into battle." - http://www.f5entertainment.com/adrenalynn/index.html


This is my first PPM for Q3A from my "Bad Girl Contest" entry. The original model weighed in at 1498 polys (1500 being the contest limit) and the extra polys were added to attempt to conceal the segmented mesh. I realize that this is a high polycount for a Q3 model, but I can honestly say that I noticed no depreciation in performance when I ran her along with 3+ bots on my system (PIII 500, 384Mb RAM, Nvidia TNT2 Ultra). 


===============================================

Notes:
The bot files were 'borrowed' and edited from the Hunter player model for Q3A. Some of the chat lines were edited by myself from direct quotes taken from the comics The Tenth and Adrenalynn: Weapon of War, written by Tony Daniel. Other chat lines were written by myself, taken from several influences and inspirational sources ;-). 

The Adrenalynn default_icon is taken from the cover of The Tenth #8, by Tony Daniel.

Skinners may notice a large area of wasted space on the addi_2 files. This is because I had initially skinned the arms asymmetrically but was unhappy with the results. So the left arm was mirrored to the right side of the body partially to save time, and partially out of laziness.
